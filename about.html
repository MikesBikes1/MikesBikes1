About Us

Founded in July 2003 in Ottawa, Mike's Bikes was born from a passion for cycling and a commitment to quality. Our shop offers a wide range of bikes and gear for all levels of cyclists, supported by expert advice and comprehensive maintenance services.

Our team is deeply integrated into the local cycling community, providing not just sales but also support through workshops, community rides, and personalized bike fitting services. We're here to help you enjoy cycling, whether you're commuting, racing, or riding for fun.
